MINECRAFT BASIC INFORMATIONS

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios. Players explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world, discovering and extracting raw materials, crafting tools, and building structures.

How to Play

The player takes an avatar that can destroy or create blocks, forming fantastic structures or digging deep to collect valuable resources.

Game Modes

Minecraft has several modes: Survival, Creative, Adventure, and Hardcore. Each offers different experiences from building to surviving.

Crafting Guide

Players can craft tools, armor, and other items to help them survive or enhance their experience.
